Your way to Secure Payments!

Safepay Accesible payments for everyone is a fun project that helps users to help themselves from being scammed by holding the transactions. If the users think the reciever is not a scammer then they can send the payment. Holding payments is a feature that help users to identify whether they are being scammed or not.
